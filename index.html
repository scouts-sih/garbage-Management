    Learn About Waste Management

    Educational resources for sustainable living

    ♻️

    Plastics

    Learn about different types of plastics and how to recycle them properly.

    • • Look for recycling codes (1-7)
    • • Rinse containers before recycling
    • • Remove caps and lids when required
    • • Avoid mixing different plastic types
    🔩

    Metals

    Metal recycling saves energy and natural resources.

    • • Aluminum cans are infinitely recyclable
    • • Rinse food cans to prevent contamination
    • • Remove paper labels when possible
    • • Steel and aluminum can be recycled together
    📄

    Paper

    Paper recycling reduces deforestation and saves trees.

    • • Keep paper clean and dry
    • • Remove plastic windows from envelopes
    • • Flatten cardboard boxes
    • • Don't recycle wax-coated paper
    🌱

    Organic Waste

    Composting organic waste creates nutrient-rich soil.

    • • Start a compost bin for food scraps
    • • Include fruit, vegetables, coffee grounds
    • • Avoid meat, dairy, and oily foods
    • • Turn compost regularly for best results
    💻

    E-Waste

    Electronic waste contains valuable materials and toxins.

    • • Never throw electronics in regular trash
    • • Find certified e-waste recycling centers
    • • Donate working electronics when possible
    • • Remove batteries before recycling

    General Tips

    • Reduce: Buy less, choose minimal packaging
    • Reuse: Find new uses for items
    • Recycle: Sort waste properly
    • Educate: Share knowledge with others
    • Advocate: Support better waste policies
